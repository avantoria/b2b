What is avantoria

Avantoria is a platform that helps your customers find splendid places through relevant feedback; yours. By suggesting your favorite cafes, restaurants or other businesses to your customers, you can ensure they enjoy an experience as great as the one in your store no matter where they are traveling to. 

who is it for

Avantoria is perfect for small businesses who seek to deepen the community of coffee enthusiasts and are tired of the existing recommendation systems where they get lost in the unfiltered amount of places. Avantoria helps businesses be found by customers that fit their target audience and fosters a community of loyal customers and other businesses.

why avantoria

The key differences to existing search and recommendation platforms are:
- A personalized community of recommenders
- Only customers themselves decide whom        they want feedback from
- No unqualified ratings such as random  feedback, bots etc.
- Be found by quality customers from your partners and other businesses

Be found by quality customers.

Not every customer shares the same values that you and your business stand for. Current search apps provide platforms for unfiltered feedback. Avantoria differs by only showing relevant feedback that is personalized and relevant. By doing so we guarantee that you will be found by the most quality customers you could imagine.

Eliminate unqualified ratings.

We at Avantoria believe that the way ratings at current search engines work do not provide much value. That is why we are eliminating this scale completely, and focus only on the recommendations that truly matter. By creating a recommendation system from coffee enthusiasts to coffee enthusiasts, Avantoria ensures a superior and delighting experience in an intuitive way.

CREATE AVANTORIA PROFILE

First things first. You create a profile on Avantoria, which will be the basis for all your activities and your connections on the platform.

CONNECT TO YOUR SOCIAL MEDIA

Connecting with your social media accounts has multiple benefits. While you are already following your favorite cafes, restaurant and other businesses, your followers are enthusiastic about your products and services, and are just waiting for benefiting from your expertise a little further. 

SETUP YOUR RECOMMENDATIONS

Setting up your recommendations is the basis for Avantoria's filtered feedback. Your link to your social media account already set the basis, and all the cafes, restaurants and other businesses you are following are already saved as potential recommendations. Browse through them one more time to decide whether your customers really should visit this place when they are traveling, and add other favorites that may not have a social media account. 

CONNECT WITH YOUR CUSTOMERS

Now that you have set up your profile, your customers can connect with you. Now your customers have access to your favorite places in your hometown and in other cities, so that no matter where they are traveling to they can enjoy a delighting experience at the high quality small businesses in town. 

EXPLORE ALL BENEFITS OF A LOYAL COMMUNITY

With your recommendations you have set up you are shaping your customers journeys no matter where they are. Besides that your shop finally receives the exposure it deserves as other small businesses will recommend you, helping attract travelers craving a superior experience to you.
