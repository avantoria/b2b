What is avantoria

Avantoria is a platform that let's your customers connect to you as one of their most trusted experts. With that connection in place, you are able to suggest cafes, restaurants or other businesses to your community and shape your customers experiences anywhere in the world. Build a community and foster loyalty.

who is it for

Avantoria is perfect for small businesses who seek a platform that allows them to engage with their most valuable customers in a new way. It helps businesses be found by customers that fit their target audience and allows to build a loyal community both with customers as well as other businesses.

why avantoria

The key differences to existing search and recommendation platforms are:
- The app is about experiences and it allows you to shape them for your customers
- Eliminate unqualified ratings such as random feedback, bots etc.
- Be found by quality customers
- It is a community for aficionados

Be found by quality customers.

Not every customer shares the same values that you and your business stand for. Current search apps provide platforms for unfiltered feedback. Avantoria differs by only showing relevant feedback that is personalized and relevant. By doing so we guarantee that you will be found by the most quality customers you could imagine.

find out more

Eliminate unqualified ratings.

We at Avantoria believe that the way ratings at current search engines work do not provide much value. That is why we are eliminating this scale completely, and focus only on the recommendations that truly matter. By creating a recommendation system from coffee enthusiasts to coffee enthusiasts, Avantoria ensures a superior and delighting experience in an intuitive way.

find out more

A community for afficionados.

Avantoria is disrupting the way small businesses reach out to customers and receive the exposure they deserve. By bringing together a select group of the highest quality places only experts can recommend, Avantoria is establishing a platform that puts small, local businesses into the spotlight. From a B2C perspective, Avantoria is maximizing the traffic of tourists and thus your customers, yet by connecting you with the best businesses that exist somewhere else, Avantoria is also shaping a new type of community among businesses. 

find out more

Loyalty at its best.

Avantoria will help you bring loyalty to the next level. Thanks to Avantoria you can connect with your customers no matter where they are. Provide valuable recommendations, curate a personal relationship or offer reward points - your options to establish a real relationship with your customers are endless.

A new way of Marketing

There is a variety of apps that show your cafe to tourists, but if you have any input on them finding your place at all it is through purchased advertising.  Avantoria is dedicated to connecting you with your existing and new customers in a completely new way, geared towards personalization and interaction. You will finally reach particularly those individuals that are looking for superior quality in a unique environment.
